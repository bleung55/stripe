Cohort win rate vs close-quarter win rate
Cohort rate = of all deals created in that quarter, how many eventually closed won. Close-quarter rate = of all deals that closed (won or lost) in that quarter, what share were wins. Divergence signals whether quarterly results are driven by current-quarter productivity or by the quality of prior pipeline. Reflects the active filter selection above.
